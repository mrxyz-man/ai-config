# Incident Response Runbook

## Trigger
Production issue, degraded service, or confirmed incident.

## Steps
1. Confirm incident scope and severity.
2. Assign incident owner and communication channel.
3. Mitigate impact (rollback, feature flag, traffic shift).
4. Collect evidence and timeline.
5. Verify recovery and close incident.

## Post-Incident
- Record root cause and follow-up tasks.
- Update monitoring and prevention controls.
